Your First Visit

What to Expect

Whether you've been following Jesus for years or you're just curious, you'll feel at home here. No pressure. No pretense. Just people encountering God together.

♫

Worship & Prayer

We begin with a time of singing, prayer, and inviting God's presence into our gathering. Expect it to feel warm, reverent, and real — not a performance.

✦

Scripture & Teaching

Our teaching is rooted in the Bible and centered on the person of Jesus. We take theology seriously and communicate it clearly — because what you believe matters.

❋

Ministry & Healing

We believe the Holy Spirit is still at work today. We regularly pray for healing, freedom, and breakthrough — because that's what Jesus did, and he told us to do the same.

"The Spirit of the Lord is upon me, because he has anointed me to proclaim good news to the poor. He has sent me to proclaim liberty to the captives and recovering of sight to the blind, to set at liberty those who are oppressed."
Luke 4:18 — Jesus, quoting Isaiah 61
Our Heart

The Lord is moving
in Dallas

People are being healed of sickness, delivered from darkness, and saved by the presence and power of Jesus. Our heart at Breakthrough Church is to partner with the Spirit to see more people experience this eternal life now.

This was Jesus' message when he announced, "The Kingdom of God is at hand." God's kingdom — his rule and his reign — is now closer than it has ever been. Jesus embodied it, the apostles established it, and we get to carry it into every corner of our city.

It doesn't matter where you've been or what you've done. Jesus loves you and has a great plan for your life. We would be honored to have you join us.

Josh Brake Lead Minister, Breakthrough Church
Who We Are

Three Pillars of Our Life Together

Everything we do flows from the presence, the teaching, and the mission of Jesus.

01

Presence of Jesus

We are a community centered on the manifest presence of God. We don't just talk about Jesus — we expect to encounter him when we gather. Through worship, prayer, and the gifts of the Spirit, we make room for God to move.

02

Discipleship to Jesus

We take the Bible seriously and study it deeply. We believe sound theology and Spirit-empowered living belong together. We seek to live obedient lives as ambassadors of his Kingdom where we live, work, and play.

03

Mission of Jesus

We minister to the world by the power and authority of the Holy Spirit. We share the gospel, pray for the sick, and bring the love of Christ to every person we meet. The harvest is plentiful — and we intend to be in the field.
